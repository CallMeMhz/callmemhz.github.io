列表初始化：列表解析 vs 列表乘法

In [1]: %timeit ['' for _ in range(1000)]
37.4 µs ± 1.64 µs per loop (mean ± std. dev. of 7 runs, 10000 loops each)

In [2]: %timeit [''] * 1000
4.94 µs ± 40.8 ns per loop (mean ± std. dev. of 7 runs, 100000 loops each)

看样子列表解析更胜一筹！

列表、字典初始化：list() 和 dict() vs [] 和

>>> from timeit import timeit
>>> timeit("[]")
0.040084982867934334
>>> timeit("list()")
0.17704233359267718
>>> timeit("{}")
0.033620194745424214
>>> timeit("dict()")
0.1821558326547077

看样子 [] {} 要比 list() dict() 要快
